Onion Burger

Description

Simple and savory burger recipe perfect for a grillout or small lunch or dinner. Easily prepped and scaled to amount of mouths to feed!

Ingredients

  • 1lbs ground beef
  • 1/4 cup teriyaki marinade sauce
  • 1(3 ounce) can french onions
  • 4 slices Cheddar cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns, split

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to high heat
  2. In a medium bowl, mix ground beef, teriyaki marinade, and french onions. Forming mixture into 4 patties
  3. Lightly oil the grilling surface, place patties on the heated grill. Grill for 4-5 minutes per side or until desired level
  4. Top with cheese and serve on buns
