Chicken Tortellini

Description

A wonderfully tasty but simple dish fit for any hearty lunch or dinner on a budget. Can be cooked with access to a air fryer or convection oven and a stove.

Ingredients

  • Fried Chicken Bites
  • Butoni Tortellini
  • Shredded Sharp Cheddar
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Begin heating a pot of boiling water large enough for your desired pasta amount
  2. Begin preheating oven to correct temperature for chicken bites
  3. Alternatively if an air fryer is available set it to the chicken setting for 13 minutes and begin to cook the chicken bites
  4. Add pasta to boiling water
  5. When chicken is finished add to a bowl and cut into smaller pieces
  6. Add a sprinkle of the shredded cheese and mayo to top of chicken
  7. Finally layer the tortellini on top of the chicken and add a final sprinkle of cheese to the top.
