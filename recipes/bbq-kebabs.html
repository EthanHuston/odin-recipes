Barbeque Kebabs

Description

Savory barbeque sausage kebabs, perfect for any family grillout in the summer heat!

Ingredients

  • 2lbs of Spicy Italian Sausage, sliced
  • 1 large bell pepper, cut into large chunks
  • 1/4lbs jalapeno peppers, cut into large pieces
  • 1 large onion, cut into large pieces
  • 1(12 fluid ounces) can of Beer
  • 1/2lbs sliced provolone cheese

Steps

  1. Place all sausage, peppers, and onions in a bowl and cover with beet to marinate for 1 hour
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil grate
  3. Alternately thread sausage, red pepper, jalapeno pepper, and onion onto a skewers
  4. Cook on prepared grill until sausage is evenly brown and veggies are tender
  5. Finally melt provolone cheese over the ingredients during final minutes
